Beef Steak

Marinate the steak in lemon juice,soy sauce,garlic,ginger and pepper for 30 minutes. Pan fry in 2 tbsp of oil add the marinated steak cook slow until done. When steak are cooked remove them from the pan onto a serving dish. Dissolve the flour with water.

Butter Chicken

Directions Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Season the chicken with salt, pepper and tarragon; drizzle with lemon juice. Place an oven proof skillet or Dutch oven over medium heat and melt the butter. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.

Pancake

Place the flour, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl, and mix well. Pour in the soy milk, maple syrup, and vanilla, and mix until smooth. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Spray the pan lightly with cooking spray. Make eight pancakes, and serve with fresh fruit, jam, soy yogurt, or maple syrup.
