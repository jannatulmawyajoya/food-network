Beef Steak

Butter Chicken

Directions Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Season the chicken with salt, pepper and tarragon; drizzle with lemon juice. Place an oven proof skillet or Dutch oven over medium heat and melt the butter. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.

Pancake

Place the flour, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl, and mix well. Pour in the soy milk, maple syrup, and vanilla, and mix until smooth. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Spray the pan lightly with cooking spray. Make eight pancakes, and serve with fresh fruit, jam, soy yogurt, or maple syrup.

Beef Picadillo Enchiladas

Prepare the quick picadillo. Heat the oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add the ground beef and fry, stirring constantly, until it is cooked through. Add the garlic, chile powder, oregano, and cumin. At this time you have a very simple picadillo that only needs a few more minutes of cooking to be completed.

Roasted Cauliflower

Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the cauliflower mixture; season with salt and pepper. Toss cauliflower mixture to coat and spread into an even layer onto a jelly roll pan. Step 3 Roast in preheated oven for about 15 minutes, turn the cauliflower, and continue roasting until soft, about 10 minute more.
